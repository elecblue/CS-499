Hi, I'm Nik

A little about myself.

It's great to meet you. My name is Nik, and I've been building things for the web for nearly 20 years. From humble beginnings customizing MySpace profiles, to working as a freelance developer, to now finally completing a formal education in Computer Science, I have nothing but passion for programming and software engineering.

While I've been primarily a JS/TS developer for the past decade, I have experience with a wider variety of languages and frameworks. Before school, I started learning Rust, and intend on sharpening my skills in systems/low-level programming now that I've completed my degree. I'm not particularly certain where I will go next, but I have always wanted to make a video game...

When I'm not staring at a screen, you can find me reading, playing video games, or exploring somewhere new with my wife and son. I love learning new things, whether it's a programming language, a game mechanic, or a hiking trail, and I'm always up for a challenge.
